📌 Reforecast vs Budget: Why the Sync Matters

The 2025 Reforecast data pulled from ABF represents near-final actuals entered by accountants — it becomes your “real” benchmark. The 2026 Budget is driven by leasing team assumptions and rent projections. This pipeline ensures both datasets are aligned for strategic review and accurate year-over-year growth analysis.

  • Bridges systems: ABF (accounting) → Yardi (leases) → Excel (reporting)
  • Supports Q4 planning, executive reviews, and board-ready summaries
  • Eliminates manual Excel work and formula mismatches
